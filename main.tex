\begin{abstract}
This report summarizes the COMETA (COST Action CA22130) workshop on polarization
tagging for hadronic boosted electroweak bosons, held in Perugia on 1--2 July
2026. The workshop brought together theorists, experimentalists, and
machine-learning experts to discuss the theoretical, experimental, and
ML-related aspects of boosted hadronic decays of $W$ and $Z$ bosons, with a
particular focus on polarization tagging and vector-boson-scattering (VBS)
topologies. The report is organized following the blocks of the workshop
agenda: semi-leptonic decay channels, jet substructure and tagging, quantum
machine learning, open-data discussion, Monte Carlo modeling of polarization,
the experimental status of polarization measurements, machine-learning
approaches, and the closing discussion towards a COMETA deliverable.
\end{abstract}
